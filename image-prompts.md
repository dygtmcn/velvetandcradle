## Style Notes for All Images:

**Aesthetic Requirements:**
- Luxury interior photography style
- Soft, natural lighting
- High-end, magazine-quality finish
- Neutral, warm color palette
- Clean, uncluttered compositions
- Emphasis on texture and material quality

**Color Palette:**
- Ivory (#faf9f6)
- Bone (#f4f4f0)
- Warm Putty (#e8ddd4)
- Greige (#c9bfb5)
- Charcoal (#2f3430)
- Natural wood tones (honey oak, maple)
- Warm brass accents

**Materials to Emphasize:**
- Natural wood (oak, maple)
- Organic cotton and linen
- Bouclé textures
- Ceramic and stone
- Unlacquered brass
- Natural fiber rugs (jute, wool)

**Lighting Style:**
- Soft, diffused natural light
- Warm color temperature
- Gentle shadows
- Golden hour quality for relevant images
- No harsh or artificial lighting
