**Genel Özellikler:**
- Boyut: 160×160px (yuvarlak crop edilecek)
- Stil: Minimal, lüks, architectural
- Renk: Warm white, cream, soft beige tones
- Konsept: Abstract design elements, not literal objects

## 🎯 **General Style Guidelines:**

### **Do:**
- Focus on abstract architectural details
- Use warm, peaceful lighting
- Emphasize luxury and quality
- Keep composition minimal and clean
- Show subtle texture and depth

### **Don't:**
- Include full furniture or rooms
- Use bright or contrasting colors
- Show people or babies
- Create literal/obvious interpretations
- Use busy or cluttered compositions

### **Technical Requirements:**
- Square format (will be cropped to circle)
- High resolution for 160px final size
- Soft, even lighting
- Clean, uncluttered background
- Professional interior design photography style

---

**Context:** These icons represent the four key principles of quiet luxury nursery design as described in the blog post "The Architecture of Quiet." Each image should capture the essence of sophisticated, serene nursery design through abstract architectural details.
